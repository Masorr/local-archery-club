Freedom

We believe in a free environment for the new, regulars and oldtimers. A local place where it's just about picking up a bow and practicing at our range. Borrow our equipment or bring your own. Have access to our instructors or chat with fellow archers.


Are you our next Robin Hood?
Come visit us at our site. Here it's totally up to you whether you want to shoot at our archery range or just relax. It's fine if you want to sit around and watch, we have some coffee and tea at our site (no food though). Make yourself at home and ask anything you'd like!

Straightforward

It's all what it's about! We believe in a simple and straightforward approach. Come to us and do some shooting! What are you waiting for? At least come by for some coffee or tea!
